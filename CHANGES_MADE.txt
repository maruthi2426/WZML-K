HOW IT WORKS NOW
================================================================================

COMMAND FLOW:
  User: /leech https://vegamovies.nf/kalinga -q 1080p
    ↓
  mirror_leech.py: Parses -q flag → self.quality = "1080p"
    ↓
  direct_link_generator(link, "1080p"): Passes quality filter
    ↓
  webscrapper_handler(link, "1080p"): Routes to scraper
    ↓
  vg.py VegamoviesScraper.scrape(link, "1080p"):
    • Fetches page with requests.get()
    • Parses HTML with BeautifulSoup
    • Finds links matching quality filter
    • Resolves shorteners to direct links
    ↓
  Returns: [{"url": "https://direct-link...", ...}]
    ↓
  Bot starts download immediately

TIME TAKEN: 5-10 seconds total
